ABOUT THE CLUB

REAL BIKERS REAL LIFE

TOTAL KMS BY THE CLUB

57,324

KMS OF RIDING EXPERIENCE

2,115,489

The Bikers Club is a community in Mumbai and Pune. Incepted in 2010, with a vision of uniting motorcycling enthusiasts, today the BC have positively impacted more than a thousand riders many – of these try not missing even a single ride! The BC do not promote any single brand of motorcycles, no matter what machine you ride, you are welcomed!

Though a chirpy bunch of riders, we do share information useful for bikers – it could be about any new safety gear, a new motorcycle being launched, how to carry luggage, how to select the right helmet, etc. Most of the information sharing happens one-on-one on the rides or over social media.

OUR BENEFITS

WHAT WE OFFER

RIDE OUTS

Bikers Club regularly organises ride-outs suit-able for all types of bikes and speeds. Our ride-outs range from short, local routes, to continental travel.

SOCIAL

With nearly 500 members, we have a very social aspect to our club. We meet every Thursday at Social Club, and regularly host various parties and events for our members.

DISCOUNTSS

All members are entitled to huge discounts from our affiliates. It's worth becoming a member just to benefit from the discounts that Bikers Club members receive!
